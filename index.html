About Us

Aravali Science Academy is a leading educational institution in Chittorgarh, Rajasthan. We have a team of well-behaved and experienced staff who are dedicated to providing the best education to our students. Our campus is equipped with CCTV surveillance to ensure the safety of our students. We also provide hostel facilities for boys and girls, and vehicle facilities within a range of 30 kilometers. We conduct monthly test series and provide foundation courses for Navodaya, Sainik School, IIT, JEE, and NEET.
